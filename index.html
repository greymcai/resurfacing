Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our website services

Do you accept South African Rands (ZAR)?

Yes, all our prices are in ZAR and we accept payments via EFT, credit card, and PayPal.

Is hosting located in South Africa for faster loading?

Yes, we use South African hosting servers for all our clients to ensure fast loading times for local visitors.

Do you provide POPIA compliance for data protection?

Absolutely. All our websites include basic POPIA compliance features, and we can provide additional compliance consulting if needed.

What payment methods do you accept in South Africa?

We accept EFT (bank transfer), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and PayPal for your convenience.

Can I cancel the monthly services anytime?

Yes, you can cancel the Professional or Premium monthly services anytime with 30 days notice. There are no long-term contracts.
